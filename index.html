Insurance Analytics

Insurance Market Analysis

This project provides a comprehensive view of insurance market performance with a strong focus on premium generation, claims analysis, profitability, and company benchmarking. The report is designed as a navigation-driven analytical application that helps users move across summary, Pareto, matrix, and information pages in a structured way.

  • Monitors earned premium, claims paid, profit, and profit margin
  • Includes Pareto analysis with manually adjustable percentage threshold
  • Supports multi-language experience and page navigation
  • Compares companies and insurance types through KPI and matrix views

Business Goal

The goal of this dashboard is to help users understand how the insurance market is performing, which companies and insurance types drive most of the revenue, and how profitability changes over time.

Analytical Value

The report combines market overview KPIs, Pareto logic, policy matrix analysis, and supporting documentation to create a complete business intelligence experience. It is especially valuable for users who need both executive summaries and deeper analytical exploration.

Technical Highlights

The solution is built on a semantic model with multiple fact tables and dimensional structures. It also includes multilingual report behavior, manual Pareto control, and intuitive icon-based page navigation.

Financial Reporting

Income Statement & Profitability Analysis

This project focuses on financial statement analysis and gives users an interactive view of operating revenue, costs, EBIT, GOP, and profitability over time. The report is structured into separate modules for statement review, monthly analysis, revenue, cost, and profitability.

  • Analyzes operating revenue, operating costs, GOP, EBIT, and net income
  • Supports monthly financial analysis with detailed account-level views
  • Includes separate revenue, cost, and profitability modules
  • Built on a semantic model designed for finance reporting and drilldown

Business Goal

The dashboard is designed to support financial performance monitoring by making the income statement easier to analyze and compare across time periods.

Analytical Value

Users can review top-level financial metrics and then move into detailed account structures, monthly P&L views, and supporting visuals to understand the drivers behind profitability changes.

Technical Highlights

The model includes financial hierarchy tables, calendar logic, and reporting structures tailored for P&L analysis. The design supports both high-level KPI consumption and detailed investigation.

Retail Analytics

Amazon Sales Analytics

This project analyzes retail sales performance through multiple perspectives including overview KPIs, regional performance, department-level analysis, and shipment behavior. It demonstrates dimensional modeling and user-friendly dashboard design for business reporting.

  • Tracks orders, quantity, sales, and profit through executive KPIs
  • Explores regional performance and customer distribution
  • Analyzes categories, departments, and shipment performance
  • Uses a dimensional model for flexible filtering and slicing

Business Goal

The main objective is to help users monitor sales performance and understand how geography, products, and shipment behavior affect profitability and business outcomes.

Analytical Value

The dashboard gives decision-makers a full retail analytics experience with high-level business metrics and supporting views that explain how performance changes across categories, regions, and time periods.

Technical Highlights

The report is built on a dimensional model and uses structured navigation across pages such as Sales Dashboard, Overview, Region, and Department. It shows strong report organization and visual consistency.

Regulatory Analytics

Financial Institutions Inspection Dashboard

This project is focused on inspection monitoring for financial institutions and supports regulatory review of inspections, penalties, cases, and institutional activity across regions. It combines operational monitoring and drill-through investigation in a single solution.

  • Monitors inspections, penalties, financial sanctions, and actions
  • Supports institution-level and regional analysis
  • Includes drill-through or popup detail for deeper investigation
  • Helps track regulatory activity through operational KPIs

Business Goal

The report helps users monitor inspection processes and enforcement outcomes while identifying trends, concentration of activity, and details behind individual cases.

Analytical Value

By combining overview pages, institution-level insights, and detailed case exploration, the dashboard makes it easier to move from operational summary to specific regulatory records.

Technical Highlights

The report uses navigation-based design, regional mapping, and interactive detail panels to support both executive monitoring and case-level investigation.

Banking Regulation

ORT Reserve Requirement & Forecast Analysis

This project analyzes reserve requirement calculations and forecasts whether banks will meet required thresholds during a reporting period. It is designed for regulatory monitoring and operational financial control.

  • Calculates required reserves using multiple regulatory components
  • Forecasts compliance with reserve requirements
  • Tracks days passed and remaining within the reporting period
  • Supports bank-level monitoring and reserve sufficiency analysis

Business Goal

The dashboard is intended to help analysts and regulators evaluate whether banks are meeting reserve-related obligations and identify potential compliance risks early in the period.

Analytical Value

The solution supports both current reserve requirement calculation and future compliance forecasting, which makes it useful for operational control and regulatory oversight.

Technical Highlights

The report uses parameter-driven calculations, reserve threshold logic, and period-based analysis to create a focused banking regulation monitoring solution.
